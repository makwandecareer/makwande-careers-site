Privacy Policy

AutoApply respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information in compliance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) of South Africa.

  • We only collect information necessary for job matching and application processing.
  • Your CV and application data are stored securely and encrypted in transit.
  • We do not share your personal information with unverified third parties.
  • You have the right to request a copy of your data or request deletion at any time.
  • We will never sell your information to marketers or advertisers.

Terms of Use

  • By using AutoApply, you consent to the collection and processing of your personal information as outlined in this policy.
  • Employers are prohibited from requesting payment from jobseekers for job opportunities.
  • AutoApply reserves the right to remove fraudulent or misleading job postings.
  • Jobseekers are encouraged to verify job offers before sharing personal or financial details.

For any questions regarding privacy or terms, please contact us at support@autoapply.com.
